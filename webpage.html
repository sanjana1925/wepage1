Meet your favorite Looney Tunes characters!

Bugs Bunny

About Bugs Bunny

Bugs Bunny is the clever and charismatic rabbit from the Looney Tunes universe. Known for his catchphrase "Eh, what's up, doc?", Bugs is a classic cartoon character loved by many. His adventures are filled with humor and wit, making him an iconic figure in animation history.

Daffy Duck

About Daffy Duck

Daffy Duck is a zany and self-absorbed duck from the Looney Tunes series. His over-the-top antics and rivalry with Bugs Bunny are legendary. Daffy's unique personality and humorous misadventures have made him a beloved character in animation.

Porky Pig

About Porky Pig

Porky Pig is known for his stuttering speech and humorous antics. As one of the earliest Looney Tunes characters, Porky's endearing personality and signature "Th-th-th-that's all folks!" make him a classic character in animation history.

Yosemite Sam

About Yosemite Sam

Yosemite Sam is a fiery gunslinger with a short temper. His constant battles with Bugs Bunny provide many hilarious moments. Sam's distinctive appearance and personality make him a memorable character in Looney Tunes.
